Instructions

Solve a board

If you have entered a board press "Start Solving" to solve the board with Backtracking

Placing numbers in cells

Click a cell and type a number from 1-9

Delete a number from a cell

To delete a number from a cell click a cell and press backspace or delete

Random board

If you don't have a solvable board you can use the inbuilt generator to generate a random board
